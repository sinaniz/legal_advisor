Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, check if your question has already been answered below.

What kind of legal information can LegalAssist AI provide?
LegalAssist AI can provide general information about various legal topics including tenant rights, employment law, family law, consumer protection, and more. However, we cannot provide specific legal advice tailored to your individual situation, as this would require a licensed attorney.
Is my conversation with LegalAssist AI confidential?
While we take data privacy seriously, conversations with our AI assistant do not have attorney-client privilege. For confidential legal advice, please consult with a licensed attorney. For more information on how we handle your data, please review our Privacy Policy.
How do I report incorrect information from the AI?
If you believe the AI has provided incorrect legal information, please contact us immediately using the form on this page or email us at support@legalassist-ai.com with details about the issue. Our legal team reviews and corrects information regularly.
Does LegalAssist AI offer services in languages other than English?
Currently, LegalAssist AI primarily operates in English, but we are working on expanding our language capabilities. Please contact us if you require assistance in another language, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.
