Bill of Sale Template

This is a basic Bill of Sale template for documenting the transfer of ownership of personal property from a seller to a buyer. Review all sections and fill in your information. Once ready, you can download the template. Please note that this template is for reference only and should be reviewed to ensure it meets your specific legal requirements.

BILL OF SALE

THIS BILL OF SALE is made on [Month Day, Year] between:

SELLER: [Seller's Full Legal Name] residing at [Seller's Full Address] ("Seller")

AND

BUYER: [Buyer's Full Legal Name] residing at [Buyer's Full Address] ("Buyer")

1. PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

The Seller hereby sells, transfers and conveys to the Buyer all rights, title and interest in and to the following described personal property ("Property"):

[Detailed description of property including make, model, year, color, serial number, VIN (for vehicles), condition, and any other relevant identifying information]

2. PURCHASE PRICE

The Buyer agrees to purchase the Property for the sum of [Amount in Words] Dollars ($[Amount in Figures]), receipt of which is hereby acknowledged by the Seller.

Payment method: [Cash/Check/Money Order/Electronic Transfer/Other]

3. AS-IS CONDITION

The Seller sells the Property to the Buyer on an "AS IS" basis. The Seller makes no warranties, express or implied, as to the condition or quality of the Property, including but not limited to its merchantability or fitness for a particular purpose, except as follows (if any):

[List any warranties or guarantees provided by the Seller, or write "NONE" if no warranties are provided]

4. SELLER'S REPRESENTATIONS

The Seller represents and warrants to the Buyer that:

(a) The Seller is the legal owner of the Property;

(b) The Property is free from all liens and encumbrances;

(c) The Seller has the right to sell the Property; and

(d) The Seller will defend the title to the Property against any and all claims and demands.

5. TITLE TRANSFER

Title to the Property shall pass from the Seller to the Buyer upon execution of this Bill of Sale and receipt of payment.

6. ODOMETER DISCLOSURE

(For motor vehicle sales only - delete if not applicable)

The Seller hereby states that the odometer reading of the vehicle described above is [Current Odometer Reading] miles/kilometers and to the best of the Seller's knowledge:

_____ This reading reflects the actual mileage of the vehicle.

_____ The odometer reading exceeds the mechanical limits of the odometer.

_____ The odometer reading is NOT the actual mileage. WARNING: ODOMETER DISCREPANCY.

7. ADDITIONAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS

[Any additional terms, conditions, or agreements between the Seller and Buyer]

8. GOVERNING LAW

This Bill of Sale shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of [State].

9. SIGNATURES

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Bill of Sale on the date first written above.

Date: [Date]

Date: [Date]
