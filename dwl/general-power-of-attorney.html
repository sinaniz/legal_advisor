GENERAL POWER OF ATTORNEY

KNOW ALL PERSONS BY THESE PRESENTS: That I, [Principal's Full Legal Name], residing at [Principal's Full Address], hereby appoint [Agent's Full Legal Name], residing at [Agent's Full Address], as my true and lawful Attorney-in-Fact (Agent).

ARTICLE I: GRANT OF AUTHORITY

I grant my Agent full power and authority to act on my behalf in all matters and to do and perform all acts, deeds, matters, and things whatsoever concerning and touching my estate, property, and affairs, as fully, effectually, and completely as I could do if personally present and acting.

ARTICLE II: POWERS GRANTED

Without limiting the generality of the foregoing, my Agent shall have the following powers (initial all that apply):

_____ Real Property Transactions: To lease, sell, mortgage, purchase, exchange, and acquire, and to agree, bargain, and contract for the lease, sale, purchase, exchange, and acquisition of, and to accept, take, receive, and possess any interest in real property whatsoever, on such terms and conditions, and under such covenants, as my Agent shall deem proper.

_____ Personal Property Transactions: To lease, sell, mortgage, purchase, exchange, and acquire, and to agree, bargain, and contract for the lease, sale, purchase, exchange, and acquisition of, and to accept, take, receive, and possess any personal property whatsoever, tangible or intangible, or interest thereto, on such terms and conditions as my Agent shall deem proper.

_____ Banking and Financial Transactions: To open, maintain, or close bank accounts, including checking, savings, and money market accounts; to conduct banking transactions; to make deposits and withdrawals; to negotiate or endorse checks or other negotiable instruments; to prepare, sign, and file tax returns.

_____ Business Transactions: To conduct, engage in, and transact all lawful business of whatever nature or kind for me and in my name; to sign, execute, acknowledge, and deliver contracts, agreements, and other obligations of whatever nature or kind related to said business.

_____ Insurance and Annuity Transactions: To exercise or perform any act, power, duty, right, or obligation whatsoever in regard to any insurance or annuity contract; to procure new, different, or additional insurance contracts; to designate the beneficiary of any such insurance contract.

_____ Estate, Trust, and Other Beneficiary Transactions: To accept, receive, receipt for, sell, assign, pledge, or exchange any legacy, bequest, devise, gift, or other interest in any estate, trust, or beneficial interest to which I am or may become entitled.

_____ Legal Actions: To commence, prosecute, discontinue, or defend all actions or other legal proceedings touching my property, real or personal, or any part thereof, or touching any matter in which I or my property, real or personal, may be in any way concerned.

_____ Personal and Family Maintenance: To hire accountants, attorneys at law, consultants, clerks, physicians, nurses, agents, servants, and other employees; to pay reasonable compensation for their services.

_____ Government Benefits: To prepare, sign and file documents, seek information, and communicate on my behalf with respect to Social Security, Medicare, Medicaid, or other governmental programs.

_____ Retirement Plan Transactions: To contribute to, withdraw from, and deposit funds into any type of retirement plan, including, but not limited to, IRAs, 401(k)s, 403(b)s, and SEP plans.

_____ Tax Matters: To prepare, execute, file, and receive tax information or returns (whether federal, state, local, or foreign), to pay taxes and receive funds due to or from any taxing authority in connection with me or my assets.

ARTICLE III: DURABILITY

This Power of Attorney shall: (initial one)

_____ Durable: Not be affected by my subsequent disability or incapacity.

_____ Springing: Become effective only upon my disability or incapacity, as certified in writing by my attending physician.

_____ Regular: Be revoked by my disability or incapacity.

ARTICLE IV: TERMINATION

This Power of Attorney shall terminate on the earlier of:

(1) [Expiration Date or "No expiration date"]

(2) Revocation by me in writing delivered to my Agent

(3) My death

ARTICLE V: THIRD PARTY RELIANCE

Third parties may rely upon the representations of my Agent as to all matters relating to any power granted to my Agent, and no person who may act in reliance upon the representations of my Agent or the authority granted to my Agent shall incur any liability to me or my estate as a result of permitting my Agent to exercise any power.

ARTICLE VI: GOVERNING LAW

This Power of Attorney shall be governed by the laws of the State of [State].

ARTICLE VII: PHOTOGRAPHIC COPIES

Photographic or other facsimile reproductions of this executed Power of Attorney may be made and delivered by my Agent, and may be relied upon by any person to the same extent as though the copy were an original.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have executed this Power of Attorney on this [Day] day of [Month], [Year].

____________________________
[Principal's Full Legal Name], Principal

ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF AGENT

I, [Agent's Full Legal Name], have read the foregoing Power of Attorney and am the person identified as the Agent. I hereby acknowledge that I accept the appointment as Agent and that when I act as Agent, I shall exercise the powers for the benefit of the Principal; I shall keep the assets of the Principal separate from my assets; I shall exercise reasonable caution and prudence; and I shall keep a full and accurate record of all actions, receipts, and disbursements on behalf of the Principal.

____________________________
[Agent's Full Legal Name], Agent
